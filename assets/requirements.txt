Databases that can be used in the implementation of SafetyNet:

1. UCF50
- University of Central Florida Human Action Database of 50 actions
-  Download link: https://www.crcv.ucf.edu/data/UCF50.rar

2. RLV [Real Life Violence Situations Dataset]
- Download link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mohamedmustafa/real-life-violence-situations-dataset/download?datasetVersionNumber=1

3. HMDB51 
- Human Motion DataBase of 51 actions
- Download link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/easonlll/hmdb51/download?datasetVersionNumber=1

UCF50 and HMDB51 might not be the most suitable for training (due to unbalanced classes)
